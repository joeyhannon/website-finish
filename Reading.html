Reading In Context.

Keeping in mind that when you read that Paul wrote to 2 groups and that not everything is addressed to saved people today, we must keep in mind that when we are reading 3 very important things.

Speaker

Who has written the text we are reading. Is it from the apostle Paul? If it is not written by Paul then it is of no use to us at all.

Audience

Who is Paul writing to in the verses. Is it Kingdom Gospel Saints, Church of the body of Christ Saints or is it unsaved people? This is important because if it is addressed to Kingdom Gospel Saints or unsaved people then it is not for us today.

Context

What is the context of the the paragraphs you are reading. What is Paul speaking about, this is important because the context shows the meaning of what is written.
